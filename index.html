About Us

MG Industries is a trusted steel fabrication company based in Erode, Tamil Nadu, with over a decade of experience in delivering high-quality structural steel solutions.

We serve clients across Southern India, including Chennai, Coimbatore, Bengaluru, Kochi, and Hyderabad, providing reliable steel fabrication services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

Our core expertise includes steel staircases, roofing structures, gates, railings, warehouse structures, and custom fabrication works.

We collaborate with experienced architectural consultants to deliver design-led, practical, and aesthetically refined steel solutions.
